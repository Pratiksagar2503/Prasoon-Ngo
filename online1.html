"Welcome to Prasoon "


‘PRASOON’ is a voluntary agency working in the areas of rural and urban development, Child education and women empowerment through advocacy, direct intervention and capacity building of the community to access the benefits of the State programs. It has been actively involved in the organizing Dalit, OBC and rural people .This community to assert their rights of equality, Health, Food and secured livelihood and respectful place in the society. With its inception in 1984 PRASOON is working with the marginalized and deprived classes of the society.
